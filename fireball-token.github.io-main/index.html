Tokenomics

The team has decided for it to be a community/charity token from launch. There will be no team or dev tokens.
Marketing is partly funded by the team and dev the rest by the community.
Transparency is key to our success.

2% Liquidity

A premium conducted on all trades leads to 2% of all transactions reflected into the liquidity pool to support the future of $FIREBALL.

2% Charity

A small percentage of each trade will be sent to a charity wallet. This wallet is protected and will only be used to donate money to charities

1% reflection

Holders do not need to stake or wait for fees to be delivered; they are immediately reflected in the holder's balance, ensuring a steady supply of passive income for all holders.

1% Burn

$FIREBALL will allow token holders to further increase income as every transaction that occurs as a token burn applied to it
